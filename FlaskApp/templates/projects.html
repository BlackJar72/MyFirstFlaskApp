Digit Span Tester: A small Java application designed to perform automated tests of human working memory. This was intended as a research tool for the fields of cognitive science and neuroscience; for example, to stimulate working memory and attention while recording brain wave activity for quantitative electroencephalographic analysis. My first useful desktop app, definitely not my best code, but it has been used in real research by several researchres.

Climatic Biomes: A an alternate biome provider written from scratch. Rather than relying heavily and random noise fields, it uses fields of attraction to areas of land/water, hot/cold, and wet dry. Large islands (“continents”) are generated based and landiness, then realistic biomes classifications are determined from climate data and game “biomes” are selected based on those.
